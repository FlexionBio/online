Developing the best therapies for osteoarthritis pain

Current therapies are either lubricant, anti-inflammatory or analgesic. Flexion Biosciences discovered that these side effects could be overcome, and made it both anti-inflammatory and analgesic, and safer for osteoarthritis pains
